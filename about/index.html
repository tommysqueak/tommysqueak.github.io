About

My name is Tom Philip, I’m a .NET/Android/Boxee/Ruby developer based in Liverpool. You can find me on about.me for all things social.

I run my own consultancy called Conker Software, offering freelance and contract based work within new media. I’m a developer with over 14 years of experience, specialising in BDD/TDD, Scrum/Lean/XP, SOLID, Usability/UX, .NET, ASP.NET MVC, Android, Boxee, Silverlight, HTML, CSS, JQuery, SQL, Ruby, Python and many more.

With the help of Lean Startup and Customer Development, I make apps and help other people make theirs. Here’s a few of mine:

  • Life Tuned - Your music memory box. Some music is timeless. When you hear it, it takes you back to a certain memory of your life. Remember those tunes.
  • Pik Pak - Create, discover and share packing lists.
  • Great Boxee - Boxee apps and developer tips.
  • Laters - For doing stuff later on Android. A Read it Later client on Android.
  • Postboxed - For finding postboxes nearby on Android.
  • XKCD Couch - For lounging on the couch flicking through XKCD comics on the TV.
