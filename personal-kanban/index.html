Personal Kanban

What else is a programmer/geek to do when he’s just moved house, a new baby is on its way (4th March) and there’s loads to do before it arrives? Set up his own Kanban board! In typical lean style, it has just the columns needed so far - TODO, Doing and Done - simple. Not imposed WIP limits as of yet!
